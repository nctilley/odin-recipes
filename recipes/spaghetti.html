Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Make the meat into meatballs
  2. Chop the tomatoes into chunks
  3. Chop the onion real good
  4. Mince that garlic
  5. Boil water and salt it generously
  6. Add noodles, cook for 11 min or until al dente
  7. Make the sauce
  8. Add the meatballs to the sauce
  9. Place the meatball and sauce combo on the noodles
  10. Enjoy
